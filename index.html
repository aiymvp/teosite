How is Greek Yogurt Made?

Pasteurization

We heat the milk to a special temperature. This is an important stage of production.

Fermentation

Starter culture is added to warm milk and kept at a specific temperature.

Filtration

The yogurt is strained to remove excess whey. This gives it a creamy and thick texture.

About TEOS

TEOS is a true Greek yogurt. It contains no stabilizers, dry whey, milk protein, dry milk, or preservatives. Just natural milk, rich taste, and high protein in every jar.
